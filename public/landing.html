About Deep life

Launch out to the deep.

It is not a strange thing to hear the statement, “African Christianity is a mile wide but an inch deep.” This is to say that African church growth focuses mostly on quantity than quality. There could be lots of energy, time and resources put into expansion, coverage, and exposure through evangelism when compared to helping someone to truly follow Christ, get grounded in the faith and take deep root in the word of God and lead a transformed, authentic and Christ-like life style in private and public arenas. As an organization, Campus Crusade for Christ seeks to produce true followers of Jesus. It received its mandate from our Lord and savior Jesus Christ to “go…and make disciples of all nations” (Matthew 28:19). In collaboration with the body of Christ, the ministry desires to contribute meaningfully in becoming a “salt and light” to the problem for the glory of God. It is out of such genuine desire that the vision of “Deep Life” has been born. Our goal is to train and equip 100, 000 leaders who shall be planting and building 100,000 churches on cities, villages, campuses, student population centers, market places, and online networks. This vision has 3 phases of implementation. Feb 10 – 31 Dec 2014 shall be a time of prayer and preparation. Jan 1 – 31 Dec 2015 will be a year of mobilization and training. Jan 1- Oct 10/2016 shall be a time of full implementation and accelerated actions across the 24 countries of the Southern and Eastern Africa in 240 locations.
